Anand Ramachandran

Co-Founder

Having worked at Accenture for 7 years, Anand decided to switch sectors and focus on using his knowledge for the social impact sphere. He did his MBA from ESADE, Barcelona and since then has worked for Oxfam, Devex and as a freelancing Innovation consultant helping NGOs and social enterprises with their strategies and product development. After noticing multiple data gaps in the humanitarian and development sectors, Anand co-founded Fields Data with a mission to give visibility to local organisations.
Born in Mumbai, Anand currently lives in Norway with his superstar wife, who also works in the same sector. Curious about almost everything in the world, you would usually find Anand experimenting in the kitchen, exploring random neighbourhoods or gobbling down hours of podcasts.
