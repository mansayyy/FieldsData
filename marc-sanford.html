Marc Sanford

Data Analyst

Shortly after completing his undergraduate degree at West Virginia University, Marc discovered that he really enjoyed working with data and crunching numbers. Having majored in political science, he was looking for an opportunity that could help him segue into a career in data analytics that was also relevant to his field of study. Marc was immediately intrigued by how Fields Data was applying data science and analytics in the fields of international development and humanitarian assistance, and began working as an intern with the company in March of 2021.  Currently, Marc is pursuing his Master of International Affairs degree, taking courses in statistics, international economics, and IT management.
  When he is not working, Marc enjoys doing creative things (such as playing the guitar), cooking, training Jiu Jitsu and other martial arts, and taking his dog Sadie for walks.
