Approach

To address the challenges shared earlier, we need to focus on:

i) getting data from places that are difficult to access
ii) keeping the data consistent by sharing and updating information regularly

We use a bottom-up approach to solicit and aggregate information from different actors through a combination of :

a) Manual - data collection with the help of on-field consultants
b) Automated - data scraping approaches on already provided online public information
"Accurate data is the lifeblood of good policy and decision-making. Obtaining it, and sharing it across hundreds of organizations, in the middle of a humanitarian emergency, is complicated and time-consuming – but it is absolutely crucial."
-United Nations Secretary-General António Guterres
